About

Get to know us Darling! Hello there! So here is a little story. Intan Nur Fasihah, founder of Kue Loyang studied business in Investment Analysis but always found a passion in food as a way of bringing together everyone she loves.

Early 2021, a year full of changes, but also a year filled with opportunities. I was craving and obsessed over sea salt chocolate chip cookies and decided to bake it by myself. After countless of try and error, I came up with a recipe that I have improvised by myself. Then, I started to bake and share with my close friends.

I always asked to myself “What’s the one item in your kitchen you can’t possibly bake without?” A tray (Loyang). There is a story with some magic attached to it along with my childhood memories and nostalgia, the aroma of baking from the kitchen, each cookie arriving on the dining table, baked with much love by my mom loving hand during Eid Ul Fitr.
